The Deadly Cost of Reporting from Gaza

The war in Gaza has been the single most deadly conflict for journalists since records began in 1981. More than five times as many journalists have been killed in Gaza than in Ukraine, and over 75% of all journalists killed in 2023 were killed in Gaza. The vast majority of those killed are Palestinian and archives of their work can be found across their social media and publishing history.
17 journalists have been killed in Gaza in 2024. This is the story of the reporters who gave their lives in 2024, to document the war and report from Gaza.
Gaza Reporters







February 23 2024

Two journalists, Mohammed Yaghi and Musab Abu Zaid, killed by an airstrike in Nuseirat Refugee Camp.
Mohamed Yaghi
30-year-old freelance photographer Mohamed Yaghi worked for multiple international outlets, including Al Jazeera. He was killed at home, along with 36 members of his family, including his wife and child.
