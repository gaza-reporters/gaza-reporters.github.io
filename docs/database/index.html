Gaza Lost Journalist Memorial

In this endless war, civilians and soldiers have died, as have some journalists seeking to document the tragedy. This database contains detailed information about war correspondents who sacrificed their lives in the Israeli-Palestinian conflict since 2000. By searching through the timeline, you can also find the latest personal information about journalists who have died since last October in the 2023 Israel-Hamas war.

There are 105 journalists, including media workers, who were killed in the conflict between Israel and the Occupied Palestinian Territory since 2000. Among them, 74 were killed during the 2023 Israel-Hamas war.
